Transfer a Product to Another Organization

Transfer a Product from one Dev Portal Organization to another.

2 mins to read

If you want to transfer your product between organizations, contact Epic Games.

To do this:

  • Create a private discussion on the support website: eoshelp.epicgames.com.

Lead Times
To ensure we transfer your product by your target date, we recommend that you contact us as soon as possible after you decide to transfer your product.

Information Required
In your message to Epic Games, include the following information:

  • The product name.
  • Your product ID.
  • The organization ID of the organization you want to transfer to.
  • A point of contact who can execute legal agreements from both organizations.
  • State whether your product has already launched on other storefronts.
  • If you have legal requirements or deadlines, please let us know and submit the information above at least 30 days before the relevant deadlines.

To find your Product ID, go to the Product Settings page in the Developer Portal. To do this:

  1. Go to the Developer Portal (dev.epicgames.com/portal).
  2. Make sure you are in the organization that you want to transfer your product from (Click on the Organization icon at the top of the left navigation to check.)
  3. In the left navigation, click on the name of the product you want to transfer. It's listed under Your Product in the left navigation.
  4. In the left navigation, click Product Settings.
  5. Scroll down to PRODUCT to see your product's product ID.

To find your Organization ID, go to the Settings tab of your Organization page. To do this:

  1. Go to the Developer Portal (dev.epicgames.com/portal).
  2. Make sure you are in the organization that you want to transfer your product to (Click on the Organization icon at the top of the left navigation to check.)
  3. In the left navigation, click Organization. (Note: You might have to click Back to organization to see this.)
  4. Click Settings in the Organization tab.
  5. See the ID under General Settings.

Next Steps
After Epic Games receives your message, the following happens:

  • An Epic Games Release Manager and Business Development contact replies to you.
  • Epic Games sends you either a Transfer Agreement or a Termination Agreement, whichever is appropriate. All parties relating to the transfer must sign the agreement before Epic Games can begin the transfer.
