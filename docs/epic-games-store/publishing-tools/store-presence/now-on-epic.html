Now on Epic

Learn how to use the Now on Epic program with your product.

Epic Games Store Only
2 mins to read

The Now On Epic program allows you to bring your previously-released products to the Epic Games Store. If you are eligible and participate, you can boost your net revenue from user spending on eligible products from 88% to 100% on eligible products in the product's first six months on the store on all payments processed by Epic Games. After the six-month run, your products continue to benefit from Epic’s 88% / 12% revenue split.

Now on Epic is open to developers and publishers on the Epic Games Store for any product released before October 31, 2023 on another third-party PC store or included in a third-party subscription service.

For more information, visit our information page.

If your product is new, you cannot include it in Now on Epic, but you might be eligible to get similar benefits from the Epic First Run program.

Process

Step 1—Get an Epic Developer Account

To create an account:

  1. Go to the Developer Portal website at dev.epicgames.com/portal and follow the instructions to sign in.
  2. Create your organization and products.

For more information, see our Developer Portal account creation guide.

Step 2—Register to Participate

  1. Log in to the Developer Portal website.

  2. Go to start a private discussion and click Create Private Discussion. Select the topic Epic Games Store and the sub-topic Now on Epic Program.

    • If you’re already a partner, you can reach out to your Epic Games Store account manager directly.

Step 3—Release your Product on the Epic Games Store

See the page Publishing Tools Workflow for more information.

Requirements and Guidelines

This program is open to developers and publishers with registered Epic Games developer accounts and eligible products that launch on the Epic Games Store on or after October 16, 2023.

For you to be eligible for Now on Epic, the following must apply:

  • You must have a product that you either:
    • Released on another third-party PC store before October 31, 2023. This includes products that you released as Early Access.
    • Included in a subscription service available on another third-party PC store before October 31, 2023.
  • If you have at least three products, then you must bring at least three of your products that are currently live on other third-party PC stores to the Epic Games Store.
  • If you have fewer than three products, then you must bring all your products that are currently live on other third-party PC stores to the Epic Games Store.
  • You must include all of your participating products in Now on Epic. However, you do not need to launch them all on the Epic Games Store at the same time.
  • You must launch all of your participating products on the Epic Games Store by June 30, 2025.
  • If you meet the above criteria, you can register for Now on Epic at any time between October 16, 2023 and December 31, 2024.
