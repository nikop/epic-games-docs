Manage Agreements

Review and accept Epic Games Store agreements

3 mins to read

Are you a Closed Beta partner? If you are not participating in the Closed Beta release, you can access documentation by using your Epic Games account to sign in to the Knowledge Base.

If you want to use the Epic Games Store publishing tools or certain optional features, your organization owner must read and accept the appropriate agreements. If your organization owner has not yet read and accepted the appropriate agreements, you will not be able to use the corresponding features of the Epic Games Store. If your organization owner is not able to accept agreements on behalf of your legal entity, please contact Service Delivery: Create a case.

  • If Epic Games makes a major version update to the End User License agreement, then you cannot use the publishing tools until your organization owner accepts the new agreement.
  • If Epic Games updates an agreement relating to a feature that you have accepted, then the Dev Portal notifies you when you try to use the self publishing tools. Your organizationâ€™s owner must accept the updated agreement to use the corresponding feature.

To review the updated agreements at any time, select Organization in the main menu, then select the Agreements tab.

The following agreements pertinent to the Epic Games Store are available for your organization to read and accept:

  • The Epic Games Store Distribution agreement
    • If your organization owner does not read and accept this agreement, then your organization cannot use the Epic Games Store Publishing Tools.
  • Epic Games Store Email Sharing agreement
    • If your organization owner does not read and accept this agreement, then your organization cannot use the optional Email Sharing feature.

Read and accept an agreement

Use this procedure to do any of the following:

  • Read and accept an agreement.
  • Read an agreement that your organization has previously accepted.
  • Download an agreement that your organization has previously accepted.

Note:

  • Any member of your organization can read an agreement. However, only your organization owner can accept one. If you need someone other than your organization owner to accept an agreement, contact Support.
  • Agreements are available only in English.
  • When you accept an agreement, we record your name, and the date and time that you signed the agreement.

To read and accept an agreement, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the Dev Portal.
  2. In the main menu , select Organization.
  3. Select the Agreements tab.
  4. For the agreement you want to read and accept, select REVIEW.
  5. Read the agreement. You must view the entire agreement before you can accept it.
  6. Optional: if you want to store an offline copy of the agreement, select Download as zip.
  7. If you accept the terms of the agreement, select Accept.

Image: To view your agreements, select Organization, then Agreements.

View the agreement history

The agreement history shows a summary of the agreements that your organization has accepted so far. It includes the following details:

  • Which agreement versions your organization has accepted.
  • Who in your organization accepted each version.
  • The date and time that your organization accepted each version.

To view the agreement history:

  1. Log in to the Developer Portal.
  2. In the main menu , select Organization.
  3. Select the Agreements tab.
  4. For the agreement whose history you want to view, select HISTORY.

Note: To view the current agreement text, see Read and accept an agreement.
