Epic First Run

Learn how to use the Epic First Run program with your product.

Epic Games Store Only
4 mins to read

The Epic First Run program is an opt-in exclusivity program for third-party developers on the Epic Games Store. If you are eligible and participate, you can boost your net revenue from user spending on eligible products from 88% to 100% in your first six months on the Epic Games Store. After the six-month run, you will continue to benefit from Epic’s 88% / 12% revenue split.

Participating products in the Epic First Run program are presented to users on-store with new exclusive badging, homepage placements, and dedicated collections (note that the exclusive badging will not be available immediately when Epic First Run launches). We will feature your products in relevant store campaigns, including sales, events, and editorial as applicable. A product benefits from continued exposure while it is in the Epic First Run Program.

The program allows developers of any scale to activate our global audience right at launch.

To get the greatest benefit from this program, you must promote your products early and often with your target audience. See our marketing tips found in our Developer Resources Documentation to learn more about publishing best practices.

The Epic First Run program is open to developers and publishers on the Epic Games Store, with eligible new releases launching on or after October 16, 2023. For more information, visit our information page.

If you have already released your product on another third-party PC store, or included it in a subscription service available on another third-party PC store, then you cannot include it in Epic First Run. However, you might be eligible to get similar benefits from the Now on Epic program.

Process

Step 1—Get an Epic Developer Account

To create an account:

  1. Go to the Developer Portal website at dev.epicgames.com/portal and follow the instructions to sign in.
  2. Create your organization and products.

For more information, see our Developer Portal account creation guide.

Step 2—Register to Participate

Note: To include your organization or product in Epic First Run, you must be one of the following:

  • The Owner of your Dev Portal organization
  • A team member with Agreements Management permissions (previously called Legal Management permissions)

Registration is open from October 16, 2023 through our self-publishing tools flow in the Dev Portal. If you use an alternate method of publishing, contact your Account Management team to register for Epic First Run.

Enroll your Organization

To include your products in Epic First Run, you must first enroll your organization in the program. To do this, a legal signatory for your organization must review and accept the Epic First Run agreement. To review and accept the agreement, in the Dev Portal navigate to Organization > Agreements.

Include your Product in Epic First Run

To include your product in Epic First Run:

  1. Navigate to Your Product > Epic Games Store > Epic First Run.
  2. Select Start enrollment.

Further Information

If you have any questions, you can either:

  • Use the Epic Games Store Developer Community to connect with our staff and other developers.
    • If you have a confidential question, log in and start a private discussion with the topic “Epic Games Store” and the sub-topic “Epic First Run”.
  • Reach out to your Epic Games Store account manager directly if you’re already a partner.

The Your Product summary page indicates whether a specific product is in Epic First Run.

Step 3—Release your Product on the Epic Games Store

See the page Publishing Tools Workflow for more information.

Requirements and Guidelines

This program is open to developers and publishers with registered Epic Games developer accounts and eligible products launching on or after October 16, 2023.

To be eligible for Epic’s First Run program, your game must meet the following requirements:

  • It must be a new product that you have not previously released on another third-party PC store or included in a subscription service on another third-party PC store.
  • You must include Epic Games Store achievements in your product.
  • After your exclusivity requirement expires, your game must be cross-play compatible with all other PC versions of the title distributed through other PC storefronts.

Products in the Epic First Run program can:

  • Release simultaneously on publishers’ and developers’ stores or launchers with direct sales.
  • Sell Epic digital redemption codes.
  • Use the Epic Games Store’s keyless redemption program to sell your products on other stores, including Green Man Gaming and Humble Store.

After the six-month exclusivity period ends, you are welcome to release your products on other third-party stores while continuing to benefit from Epic’s 88% / 12% revenue share.

The following are not eligible:

  • Products that have previously been released on another third-party PC store.
  • Products that are included in a subscription service available on another third-party PC store.
  • Products or apps with a pre-existing exclusivity deal with the Epic Games Store.
  • Products that are not in compliance with Epic Games Store’s distribution and content guidelines.

You can unenroll from Epic First Run at any point prior to your game launching on the store. When your product is live, you are no longer able to unenroll.
