Create and configure your product

How to create your product in KWS and provide some basic information for integration with KWS services.

2 mins to read

NOTE: This section is all about setting up your product(s) in KWS. To delete a product from KWS, contact us to request the deletion.

Create your first product

The initial KWS registration flow prompts you to create your first product, as described here. By default, your first product is created in the Test environment.

  1. Enter your product name and click Create product:

    Click image for full size.

    The Dashboard opens, showing your new product:

    Click image for full size.

  2. To create additional products, click the Add product button in the top right corner:

    Click image for full size.

  3. Click on a product to complete its configuration, as described next.

Configure your product

KWS services need to display your product's name, privacy policy URL, and customer support URL. To supply these values to KWS:

  1. Under Basic information, review the Product name. This is the name that KWS displays in KWS service webviews and emails. It can be different from the name that you used to create the product.

    Restrictions:

    • No inappropriate words.
    • No blatant plagiarism or copyright violation.

  2. In the Product URLs, panel, enter the URL for your Product's privacy policy. KWS displays a link to this URL in KWS service webviews and emails:

    Click image for full size.

    Restrictions:

    • Your privacy policy must exist at the provided link.
    • The privacy policy must make sense and not be meaningless content.

  3. In Customer's support/FAQs, enter the URL for your customer support or FAQs site. KWS displays a 'please let us know' link to this URL in KWS service emails:

    Click image for full size.

    Restrictions:

    • Your customer support/FAQs page must exist at the provided link.
    • The FAQs must make sense and not be meaningless content.
