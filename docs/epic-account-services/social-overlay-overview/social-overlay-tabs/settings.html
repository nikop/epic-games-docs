Settings

Details on what a player can do on the Settings tab of the Social Overlay.

2 mins to read

This documentation assumes that you install the Social Overlay with your game and initialize the EOS SDK when a player starts your game. For more information on how to install and initialize the Social Overlay, see the SDK Integration documentation.

Image: The Settings tab of the Social Overlay, from Fall Guys (EOS SDK 1.15).

The player can navigate to the Settings tab on the Social Overlay and customize their experience of notifications and friend requests.

When a player uses the Settings tab, what they can do depends on the version of the EOS SDK that you use for your game. See the sections below on each SDK version for more information.

Settings with EOS SDK 1.14

When you use EOS SDK 1.14 for your game, the player can use the Settings tab to do the following:

  • Turn on the Do Not Disturb toggle to disable all notifications. This means the player no longer sees notifications sent by the Social Overlay.

Settings with EOS SDK 1.15

When you use EOS SDK 1.15 for your game, the player can use the Settings tab to do the following:

  • Turn on the Do Not Disturb toggle to disable all notifications. This means the player no longer sees notifications sent by the Social Overlay.

  • From the Receiving Friend Requests drop-down menu, select one of these options to choose who can send friend requests to the player:

    • Anyone: Anyone with an Epic Games account can send friend requests to the player.
    • Friends of Friends: Only the player’s friends of friends can send friend requests to the player. For more information on how a friend is defined, see What is a Friend?
    • No One: The player cannot receive friend requests on the Social Overlay.

    For more information about players sending and receiving friend requests on the Social Overlay, see Friends.

Note: If the player is under the age of digital consent and their account requires a Guardian PIN, then they have to enter the Guardian PIN to send or accept invitations. See the Epic Games Cabined Accounts documentation for more information.

Settings with EOS SDK 1.16

When you use EOS SDK 1.16 for your game, the player can use the Settings tab to do the following:

  • Turn on the Do Not Disturb toggle to disable all notifications. This means the player no longer sees notifications sent by the Social Overlay.

  • From the Receiving Friend Requests drop-down menu, select one of these options to choose who can send friend requests to the player:

    • Anyone: Anyone with an Epic Games account can send friend requests to the player.
    • Friends of Friends: Only the player’s friends of friends can send friend requests to the player. For more information on how a friend is defined, see What is a Friend?
    • No One: The player cannot receive friend requests on the Social Overlay.

    For more information about players sending and receiving friend requests on the Social Overlay, see Friends.

Note: If the player is under the age of digital consent and their account requires a Guardian PIN, then they have to enter the Guardian PIN to send or accept invitations. See the Epic Games Cabined Accounts documentation for more information.
