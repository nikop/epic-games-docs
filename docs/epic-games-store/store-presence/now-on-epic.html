Now on Epic

Find out how to use the Now on Epic program with your product.

Epic Games Store Only
2 mins to read

Use the Now On Epic program to bring your previously-released products to the Epic Games Store. If you are eligible and participate, you can boost your net revenue from user spending on eligible products from 88% to 100% on eligible products in the product's first six months on the store on all payments processed by Epic Games. After the six-month run, your products continue to benefit from Epicâ€™s 88% / 12% revenue split.

Eligibility

Now on Epic is open to developers and publishers on the Epic Games Store for any product released before October 31, 2023 on another third-party PC store or included in a third-party subscription service.

For more information, see:

  • store.epicgames.com/~Now On Epic.
  • The Requirements and Guidelines section of this page.

If your product is new, you cannot include it in Now on Epic, but you might be eligible to get similar benefits from the Epic First Run program. For information, see documentation [Epic First Run]](/epic-games-store/store-presence/epic-first-run).

Process

After you have set up an Epic Games account, your organization, and your product on the Epic Games Store, you can register to participate in Now on Epic.

You set up an Epic Games account, your organization, and your product in the Developer Portal as part of the publishing tools onboarding process. (See documentation: Publishing Tools Workflow - Onboarding - Process.)

Enroll your Organization Now on Epic

Contact Epic Games support to enroll. To do this:

  1. Create a private discussion on: eoshelp.epicgames.com.
  2. Select the topic Epic Games Store and the sub-topic Now on Epic Program.

Note: If you use an alternate method of publishing, contact your Account Management team to register for Now on Epic.

Launch Your Product on the Epic Games Store

After you have included your product in Now on Epic, you can launch your product on the store, as part of the Now on Epic program. You launch your product as part of the publishing tools process. Documentation: Publishing Tools Workflow.

Requirements and Guidelines

This program is open to developers and publishers with registered Epic Games developer accounts and eligible products that launch on the Epic Games Store on or after October 16, 2023.

For you to be eligible for Now on Epic, the following must apply:

  • You must have a product that you either:
    • Released on another third-party PC store before October 31, 2023. This includes products that you released as Early Access.
    • Included in a subscription service available on another third-party PC store before October 31, 2023.
  • If you have at least three products, then you must bring at least three of your products that are currently live on other third-party PC stores to the Epic Games Store.
  • If you have fewer than three products, then you must bring all your products that are currently live on other third-party PC stores to the Epic Games Store.
  • You must include all of your participating products in Now on Epic. However, you do not need to launch them all on the Epic Games Store at the same time.
  • You must launch all of your participating products on the Epic Games Store by June 30, 2025.
  • If you meet the above criteria, you can register for Now on Epic at any time between October 16, 2023 and December 31, 2024.
