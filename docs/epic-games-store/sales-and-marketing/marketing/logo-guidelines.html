Logo Guidelines

Learn about the requirements for logos on the Epic Games Store.

Epic Games Store Only
4 mins to read

If you are using self-service Epic Games Store publishing tools, any changes or updates related to your Product Setup or Product Home pages should be made through the Developer Portal. If you need assistance, search or post in the Epic Developer Community forums.

For partners using an alternate method of distribution, please email your Epic Games Store account team for help.

As part of product marketing best practice, you need to make sure users are aware of where the product is available. This applies as soon as you announce the product.

To do this, create marketing materials that clearly indicate where your product is available so that they can take action (for example, by wishlisting your product). Don’t assume that users will automatically know where to find it. For PC, we recommend that you use the logos of each platform. instead of the generic ‘PC’ logo. to ensure clarity about the availability of your game, as you would for consoles.

  • More information about marketing best practices: Product Marketing Tips

On this page, you will learn how to use the Epic Games Store logo when you create marketing assets for your product. These assets are materials such as trailers, banners, displays, as well as announcements across your communications channels such as your product web page, newsletters and social channels.

Before You Begin

Before you use the Epic Games Store logo, review the following guidelines and legal requirements.

  • Further information: Legal Requirements

When you mention the PC platform, use the Epic Games Store logo, not the Epic Games logo.

The Epic Games Store logo exists in the following versions:

  • Vertical and horizontal logos that you can use depending on the layout where you want to place the logo.
  • A black and white logo that you can use depending on the color of the background. More information: Background Color
  • There is also a smaller vertical logo for use when you need smaller sizes. For example, this is useful when you want to put the Epic Games Store logo with several other logos in your design.

How to Place the Epic Games Store Logo Near Other Logos

Vertical Logo

Use the vertical logo in applications where there is more vertical space available or when you do not use full wordmarks.

Horizontal Small Logo

Use the horizontal small logo to increase legibility at smaller sizes or when vertical space is limited.

Spacing

Ensure there is adequate spacing between the Epic Games Store logo and any other logo. Use a minimum spacing equal to the width of the Epic Games shield.

Usage

Background Color

Ensure there is enough contrast between background colors and the logo, as follows:

  • If you want to place the logo on a light background, use the black logo.
  • If you want to place the logo on a dark background, use the white logo.
  • If you want to place the logo over busy images, fade the image to improve the legibility of the logo.
    • If you can't fade the image, place the logo over the least busy area of the background.
  • If you want to place the logo on top of colors or image backgrounds, use the PNG logo so the color and image can be seen through the text.

Logo Don'ts

The Epic Games Store team understands the temptation to make adjustments to fit your design. However, the team asks that you do not alter the Epic Games Store logo.
