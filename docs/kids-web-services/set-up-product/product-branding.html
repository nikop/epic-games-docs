Apply your branding to your KWS integration

How to customize the appearance of KWS services to match your product's branding.

3 mins to read

The Branding tab

The Branding tab enables you to customize the appearance of KWS services integrated with your product so that they match your brand and provide a seamless experience for your users. You can customize the banner, favicon, font, button color, and theme that KWS services use for emails and webviews.

To get started, click the Dashboard tab, select the required product , and then click the Branding tab.

As you edit the branding properties, the preview on the right-hand side automatically refreshes to demonstrate your changes:

Banner image

The banner image appears at the top of all KWS service emails and webviews. If you do not specify a custom banner image, KWS displays a Kids Web Services banner, as shown in the default preview.

To upload a custom banner image, drag the image from your file browser onto the dotted rectangle.

Alternatively, click on the dotted rectangle to open your file browser, select the required image, and then click Open.

The preview on the right-hand side automatically displays the custom banner.

To change your custom banner image:

  1. Click on the existing image or click Edit. The file browser opens.
  2. Select the required image, or drag it onto the existing image.

Restrictions:

  • The uploaded banner image file must be a jpeg or png file.
  • The image must be 720 x 250 pixels (width x height).
  • No blatant plagiarism or copyright violation.
  • The image must not be obscene.
  • The image must have some direct relation to your product or organization.

Favicon

The favicon appears on the browser tab of KWS service webviews. If you do not specify a custom favicon, KWS displays the Kids Web Services favicon.

To configure a custom favicon, drag an image from your file browser onto the dotted rectangle.

Alternatively, click on the dotted rectangle to open your file browser, select the required image, and then click Open.

To change your custom favicon:

  1. Click on the existing image or click Edit. The file browser opens.
  2. Select the required image, or drag it onto the existing image.

Restrictions:

  • The uploaded favicon image file must be a jpeg or png file.
  • The image must be 512 x 512 pixels (width x height).
  • No blatant plagiarism or copyright violation.
  • The image must not be obscene.
  • The image must have some direct relation to your product or organization.

Primary color

This is the color that KWS uses for buttons in KWS service emails and webviews. It defaults to blue (R=41, G=138, B=232).

To specify or change a custom primary color, click on the colored button. The color picker is displayed:

Click image for full size.

Use the color picker to select a new color. As you change the color, the preview on the right-hand side automatically demonstrates the custom color.

Font

KWS uses this font in KWS service webviews. The default font is 'Open Sans'.

NOTE: KWS service emails use a standard web font, which you cannot customize.

To change the font for KWS service webviews, select a font from the dropdown menu. The preview on the right-hand side automatically demonstrates the selected font.

Theme

The theme is the background shade of KWS service emails and webviews. The available options are 'Light Theme' and 'Dark Theme'. The default is 'Light Theme'.

To change the theme, select from the dropdown menu. The preview on the right-hand side automatically demonstrates the selected theme.

Language

When calling the KWS API, you can specify the language that KWS uses in KWS service emails and webviews. See Localization in KWS.
