Guides

Storefront Media Guide

This guide contains specifications and recommendations for storefront media assets, such as your product logo, offer images, videos, and carousel images. It also provides some examples of different areas where assets can show up on the storefront. This resource is intended for partners who use our self-publishing tool.

Learn more: Storefront Media Guide

Epic Games Store Brand Guidelines

This is a more streamlined version of Storefront Media Guide, aimed at user experience or marketing designers who may not be working in the Developer Portal. The Storefront Asset section has information specific to Epic Games Store.

Learn more: Epic Games Store Brand Guidelines

EAS Design Guidelines

If you are integrating with EOS Epic Account Services Authentication, we have specific design guidelines for the sign-on experience with Epic Account Services. Using the standard Epic Games Sign-In button is strongly recommended as it enables your users to identify the Epic Games option more quickly.

Learn more: Design Guidelines

Additional Resources

Content Guidelines

To promote an optimal store experience to users, the Epic Games Store maintains a number of guidelines that apply to both product and storefront content. During the review of your product prior to launch, the Epic Games Store team will ensure these guidelines have been followed. This contains policies specific to Media Assets.

Learn more: Content Guidelines

Manage Offers

This article outlines The Offers page of the Dev Portal. The Images section of Dev Portal is where you will upload your digital assets used to represent and market your product throughout the storefront.

Learn more: Manage Offers
