The KWS Developer Portal

An overview of KWS' developer console.

Under 1 min to read

Click here to open the KWS Developer Portal.

The Developer Portal is KWS' web-based self-serve management console. It enables you to manage your KWS accounts and set up the PV service on your products:

Click image for full size.

The main menu options are as follows:

  • Dashboard - Enables you to create, view, and edit your product(s), and set up the PV service on them.
  • Organization - Enables you to manage your KWS organization details and team membersâ€™ accounts.
  • Documentation - Opens this documentation in a new tab.
  • Support - Opens the Epic Online Services developer support site in a new tab.

The KWS Developer Portal works with any modern browser.
