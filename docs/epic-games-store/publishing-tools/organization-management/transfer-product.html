Transfer a Product to Another Organization

Transfer a Product from one Dev Portal Organization to another.

Under 1 min to read

You might want to transfer a product to another organization due to license transfers, merging developer resources, or other business reasons.

To begin the process, create a private discussion on our Developer Support site. To ensure that the transfer happens by your target date, we recommend that you reach out as soon as possible when you decide to transfer the product to another organization.

In your message, include the following information:

  • The product name.
  • The product ID.
    • To find your Product ID, in the Dev Portal, go to Your Product > Product Settings.
  • The organization you want to transfer the product to.
    • To find your Organization ID, in the Dev Portal, go to Your Dashboard > Organization > Settings.
  • A point of contact who can execute legal agreements from both organizations.
  • State whether your product has already launched on other storefronts.
  • If you have legal requirements or deadlines, please let us know and submit the information above at least 30 days before the relevant deadlines.

After we receive your message, the following happens:

  • An Epic Games Release Manager and Business Development contact reach out to you.
  • Epic Games sends you a Transfer Agreement or a Termination Agreement as appropriate, which must be signed by all parties to begin the transfer.
