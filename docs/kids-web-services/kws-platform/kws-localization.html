Localization in KWS

Supported languages, and how to localize your KWS service.

Under 1 min to read

Languages supported by the KWS platform

  • The KWS Developer Portal is available only in English.
  • This documentation is available in English, Korean, Japanese, and Simplified Chinese.

Languages supported by the Parent Verification service

You can localize the PV service so that all parent-facing verification emails and webviews are in the parent's language. To do this, pass the required language code in your call to the KWS API.

The PV service supports these languages:
Arabic ar Korean ko
Chinese (Simplified) zh-Hans Polish pl
Dutch nl Portuguese (Brazil) pt-BR
English en Portuguese (Portugal) pt
Filipino (Tagalog) tl Russian ru
French fr Spanish es
German de Thai th
Italian it Turkish tr
Japanese ja
