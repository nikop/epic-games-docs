Brand Guidelines Overview

Resources for your assets.

Epic Games Store Only
2 mins to read

If you are using self-service Epic Games Store publishing tools, any changes or updates related to your Product Setup or Product Home pages should be made through the Developer Portal. If you need assistance, search or post in the Epic Developer Community forums.

For partners using an alternate method of distribution, please email your Epic Games Store account team for help.

Guides

Storefront Media Guide

This guide contains specifications for the assets (such as logos and product images) that you must use on your product display pages (PDPs).

Example of a Product Home Page: https://store.epicgames.com/p/fortnite

More information:

  • Full guide: Storefront Media Guide
  • How to set up your Product Home Page, and create, edit, and view offers associated with your product: Manage Offers.

Content Guidelines

To promote an optimal store experience to users, the Epic Games Store applies a set of guidelines to product and storefront content. During the review of your product prior to launch, the Epic Games Store team ensures that you have followed these guidelines. This contains policies specific to media assets.

More information:

  • Media asset policies
  • Full guidelines: Content Guidelines

Epic Games Store Logo Guidelines

These guidelines explain how to use the Epic Games Store logo when you create marketing assets for your game, such as trailers, banners and displays. The guidelines also apply across your communication channels such as your game webpage, newsletters and social channels.

More information:

  • Full guidelines: Epic Games Store Logo Guidelines
  • Before you use the Epic Games Store logo, review our legal requirements.

Epic Games Store Marketing Guidelines

For details of the various Epic Games Store marketing channels and the formats and guidelines of the marketing assets used across these channels, see Marketing Overview.

If the Epic Games Store marketing team would like to use marketing content related to your product on its channels, it will liaise with you accordingly.

Epic Account Services Design Guidelines

If you are integrating with EOS Epic Account Services Authentication, there are specific design guidelines for the sign-on experience with Epic Account Services. Using the standard Epic Games Sign-In button is strongly recommended as it enables your users to identify the Epic Games option more quickly.
