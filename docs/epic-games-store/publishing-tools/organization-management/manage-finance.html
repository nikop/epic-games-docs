Manage Trader, Tax, and Payout Details

Provide required financial information for your organization.

5 mins to read

Overview

Before you can distribute your products on the Epic Games Store, you must go through trader verification, create a tax profile, and activate your payout account. This process can take two weeks or more to complete beause the information provided must undergo verification. You cannot submit your product for review by the Epic Games Store team, which is a necessary part of the publishing workflow, until you complete this process.

Ensure the financial information you provide throughout this process reflects the entity type you select. For example, if you select "company" or "business" as your entity type, provide the requested information (such as IDs) for your organization rather than personal information.

Complete Trader Verification

Before you can sell products on the Epic Games Store, you must put your Epic Games Store organization through trader verification. This process involves the following:

  • You commit to only offer products or services on the Epic Games Store that comply with the applicable rules of the European Union law.
  • You provide trader information that the Epic Games Store verifies. The Epic Games Store shares some of this information with consumers as part of the Epic Games Store Publisher Index.
    • The Publisher Index is a list of publishers on the Epic Games Store, and the products they sell. View the Epic Games Store Publisher Index.

Note:

  • If your Epic Games Store organization is new to the Epic Games Store, then you must put the organization through trader verification before you can publish products on the Epic Games Store.
  • If your organization is already on the Epic Games Store, then you have until February 17, 2025 to put the organization through trader verification. If you do not do so by this date, then you will not be able to publish products on the Epic Games Store. In addition, Epic Games Store will remove your existing live products.

To complete trader verification, follow the steps below.

  1. In the Developer Portal (Dev Portal), go to Organization > Trader Info.
  2. Click Start trader verification.
  3. Follow the prompts to confirm that you are a trader, and to provide the relevant contact details to Epic Games.
  4. When you return to the Trader Info tab, click Add Trader Record.
  5. Follow the prompts to provide the relevant information.
  6. Wait for Epic Games Store to carry out your trader verification. This usually takes an hour or less. When verification is complete, Epic Games sends you a notification email.
  7. In the Dev Portal, go to Organization > Trader Info.
  8. Answer the questions about your address and telephone number.

If Your Trader Verification Succeeds

The Trader Info tab shows a preview of your Verified Trader card. The Epic Games Store displays this information in the Epic Games Store Publisher Index.

If Your Trader Verification Fails

The Trader Info tab displays a message that your trader verification failed. To retry, you must go through the verification process again. To do so, contact Epic Games Store support with a private discussion.

Edit Your Trader Information

If you have gone through trader verification and want to edit the information shown on the Trader Info tab, do the following:

  • You can change the options Display my address publicly and Display my phone number publicly on the tab. Click Save Changes to apply your changes.
    • Note: You cannot change the answers that you gave during verification about your home or personal address and home or personal phone number. To change these answers, you need to contact Epic Games Store support with a private discussion.
  • To change any other trader information, you must go through verification again. To do so, contact Epic Games Store support with a private discussion.

Create Tax Profile

Create your tax profile by following the steps below. You must complete this step before you can activate your payout account.

  1. In the Dev Portal, navigate to Organization > Tax Info.
  2. In the Start Tax Interview section, select the appropriate entity type for your tax profile (e.g., personal, business, etc.). Then, click Start Tax Interview.
  3. Follow the prompts to provide the required tax information for your entity type, which may include identity and location information, tax status, and applicable tax forms. On the final step, click Exit Interview to return to your dashboard.

After the interview is complete, your tax profile is automatically submitted for verification. Depending on your location, the verification process may take up to 5 days or more. You can monitor the status of your profile at any time by visiting the Tax Info tab.

When your profile is verified, an email containing next steps is sent to the organization owner, and an overview of your profile displays on the Tax Info tab. You can then proceed to the Activate Payout Account process.

Tip: To update your tax profile after creation, select the Retake Tax Interview button available on the Tax Info tab of the Dev Portal.

Activate Payout Account

Once your tax profile has been created, you can activate your payout account through Hyperwallet. To do so, follow the steps below.

Some organizations, such as those based in Turkey, do not need to create a Hyperwallet account. Instead, you will complete an alternate registration process to provide your payout account information. After completing the tax interview, visit the Payout Info tab for details on how to proceed.

  1. Ensure a tax profile has been created and verified for your organization.
  2. In the Dev Portal, navigate to Organization > Payout Info and click Activate Hyperwallet Account to launch the activation process on the Hyperwallet site.
  3. Follow the prompts on the Hyperwallet site to create your account. During this process, you will be asked to verify your payee information by supplying identity and/or business registration documentation. This step is required in order to complete the activation.
  4. On the final step of the Hyperwallet process, click Submit to finalize your account.

If you encounter any issues during this process, review the Epic Games Hyperwallet FAQ for assistance.

Once the account is finalized, your information is automatically submitted for verification. Depending on your location, the verification process may take up to 7 days or more. You can monitor the status of your account at any time through the Hyperwallet site dashboard.

Once your information is verified, a confirmation email is sent to the Hyperwallet account owner. Additionally, an overview of your account displays on the Payout Info tab of the Dev Portal.

Tip: To update your Hyperwallet account after creation, navigate directly to the Hyperwallet site or select the Update Hyperwallet Account button available on the Payout Info tab.
