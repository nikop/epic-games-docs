Configure the redirect URL

How to configure a redirect URL to which KWS sends parents after verification.

Under 1 min to read

The Redirect URL setting is optional. If entered, KWS redirects the parent to this URL after they successfully verify their identity. For example, you might wish to redirect the parent to a webpage where they can manage their childâ€™s permissions in your app.

If you do not specify a redirect URL, KWS displays a verification success message within the PV service web page:

  1. To get to your PV service settings, select your product in the Dashboard, then click the Services tab, and in the Parent Verification panel, click View or Edit.
  2. In your PV service settings, enter your Redirect URL.

Restrictions:

  • The redirect URL must be in this format: https://your.domain.com/your/path
  • A valid web resource must exist at the provided URL.
  • Your redirect URL should not be used to determine the result of the verification. You should use a webhook to determine the result.

NOTE: The information in the redirect URL is not signed and may be modified by the parent.
