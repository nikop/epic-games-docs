Create Custom Price Tiers

Create price points outside default suggested pricing

5 mins to read

Are you a Closed Beta partner? If you are not participating in the Closed Beta release, you can access documentation by using your Epic Games account to sign in to the Knowledge Base.

Overview

When creating offers for your product, you set a base USD price tier for those offers. Default price tiers for additional regions are then calculated using the provided USD value. If needed, you can create custom price tiers to determine your own regional pricing in place of the suggested price tiers.

Custom price tiers must be approved by the Epic Games Store team before you can add them to your offers.

Create Custom Price Tier

To create a custom price tier, follow the steps below:

  1. In the Dev Portal, navigate to Your Product > Epic Games Store > Custom Price Tiers.
  2. Click Create Price Tier. Then, enter a name and base USD price for the tier.
  3. In the Regional Base Prices section, enter the desired price for each region as needed.
  4. Once the custom prices are set, click Submit to send your new price tier for review.

Once your custom price tier is approved by the Epic Games Store team, it becomes an available price option when configuring offers. Custom price tiers are not associated with any offers by default.

Add Custom Price Tier to Offer

To add your custom price tier to an existing offer, follow the steps below. To add the custom price tier to a new offer, follow the standard process for creating offers.

  1. Navigate to Your Product > Epic Games Store > Offers and select the offer you wish to update.
  2. On the Offer Details screen, click Price.
  3. In the Base Price Tier drop-down, select your new custom price tier.
  4. Review your update, then click Save Changes.
