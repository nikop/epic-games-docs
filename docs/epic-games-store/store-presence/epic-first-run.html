Epic First Run

Find out how to use the Epic First Run program with your product.

Epic Games Store Only
5 mins to read

The Epic First Run program is an opt-in exclusivity program for developers on the Epic Games Store. If you are eligible and participate, you can boost your net revenue from user spending on eligible products from 88% to 100% in your first six months on the Epic Games Store. After the six-month run, you will continue to benefit from Epic’s 88% / 12% revenue split.

Participating products in the Epic First Run program are presented to users on-store with the following benefits:

  • Exclusive badging
  • Homepage placements
  • Dedicated collections

Epic Games features your products in relevant store campaigns, including sales, events, and editorial as applicable. A product benefits from continued exposure while it is in the Epic First Run Program.

The program allows developers of any scale to activate Epic's global audience right at launch.

To get the most benefit from this program, promote your products early and often with your target audience. For guidance on publishing best practices, see documentation: Product Marketing Tips.

Eligibility

The Epic First Run program is open to developers and publishers distributing on the Epic Games Store, with eligible new releases launching on or after October 16, 2023.

For more information, see:

  • store.epicgames.com/~ Epic First Run
  • The Requirements and Guidelines section of this page.

If you have already released your product on another third-party PC store, or included it in a subscription service available on another third-party PC store, then you cannot include it in Epic First Run. However, you might be eligible to get similar benefits from the Now on Epic program. For information, see documentation: Now on Epic.

Process

After you have set up an Epic Games account, your organization, and your product on the Epic Games Store, you can register to participate in Epic First Run.

You set up an Epic Games account, your organization, and your product in the Developer Portal as part of the publishing tools onboarding process. (See documentation: Publishing Tools Workflow - Onboarding - Process.)

Note: If you use an alternate method of publishing, contact your Account Management team to register for Epic First Run.

Enroll your Organization in Epic First Run

To include your products in Epic First Run, you must first enroll your organization in the program. To do this, a legal signatory for your organization must review and accept the Epic First Run agreement.

Permissions Required
To enroll your organization and include your product in Epic First Run, your Epic Games Account must have the following role or permissions:

  • Role: Owner of your organization (Documentation: Manage Your Organization).
  • Permissions: An organization team member with Agreements Management permissions (previously called Legal Management permissions).

Accept the Agreement
To review and accept the agreement, in the Developer Portal, go to the Agreements tab on the Organization page.

To do this:

  1. In the Developer Portal (dev.epicgames.com/portal), make sure you are in the correct organization relating to your product.
    To do this:
    • Select Organization from the top of the left navigation’s dropdown menu.
    • Follow the onscreen instructions to choose your organization.
  2. On the left navigation, click Organization. (Note: You might have to click Back to organization to see this.)
  3. On the Organization page, click Agreements.
  4. On the Agreements tab, scroll down to the Epic First Run Agreement tile and click Review.
  5. Follow the onscreen instructions to accept the agreement. Note that you can't click Accept until you have scrolled through the whole agreement.

Include your Product in Epic First Run

After you have enrolled your organization in Epic First Rub by accepting the agreement, you can include your product in Epic First Run. To do this, go to the Epic First Run page on the Developer Portal.

To do this:

  1. In the Developer Portal (dev.epicgames.com/portal), make sure you are in the correct organization relating to your product.
    To do this:
    • Select Organization from the top of the left navigation’s dropdown menu.
    • Follow the onscreen instructions to choose your organization.
  2. On the left navigation, click Organization. (Note: You might have to click Back to organization to see this.)
  3. On the left navigation, under Your Product, click the name of your product.
  4. On the left navigation, click Epic Games Store and then Epic First Run.
  5. Click Start enrollment and follow the onscreen instructions.

The Your Product summary page indicates whether your product is in Epic First Run.

Launch your Product on the Epic Games Store

After you have included your product in Epic First Run, you can launch your product on the store, as part of the Epic First Run program. You launch your product as part of the publishing tools process. Documentation: Publishing Tools Workflow.

Requirements and Guidelines

This program is open to developers and publishers with registered Epic Games developer accounts and who have eligible products launching on or after October 16, 2023.

To be eligible for Epic’s First Run program, your game must meet the following requirements:

  • It must be a new product that you have not previously released on another third-party PC store or included in a subscription service on another third-party PC store.
  • You must include Epic Games Store achievements in your product.
    Documentation: Achievements.
  • After your exclusivity requirement expires, your game must be crossplay compatible with all other PC versions of the title distributed through other PC storefronts.
    Documentation: Cross-Platform Multiplayer Guidelines - Crossplay Requirements.
  • Note that blockchain, distributed ledger, NFT, or other similar products cannot participate: the Epic Game Store terms do not allow this type of product to use Epic-administered methods.
    For further information, see the Epic Games Store Blockchain Rider agreement in the Agreements tab of your Organization page in the Developer Portal, and the following documentation: Blockchain Technology Guidelines.

Products in the Epic First Run program can participate in the following ways:

  • Release simultaneously on publishers’ and developers’ stores or launchers with direct sales.
  • Sell Epic digital redemption codes.
  • Use the Epic Games Store’s keyless redemption program to sell your products on other stores, including Green Man Gaming and Humble Store. Documentation: Publishing Process - Access Keys - Keyless Integration.

After the six-month exclusivity period ends, you are welcome to release your products on other third-party stores while continuing to benefit from Epic’s 88% / 12% revenue share.

The following products are not eligible:

  • Products that have previously been released on another third-party PC store.
  • Products that are included in a subscription service available on another third-party PC store.
  • Products or apps with a pre-existing exclusivity deal with the Epic Games Store.
  • Products that are not in compliance with Epic Games Store’s distribution and content guidelines.
    Documentation: Requirements and Guidelines.

You can unenroll from Epic First Run at any point prior to your game launching on the store. When your product is live, you are no longer able to unenroll.
