Guidelines & Specifications

Featured Carousel Specs: Option 1: Static Image

Example highlighted in red:

  • Sizes:
    • Desktop Image: 1248 x 702 px: no logo, no text
    • Thumbnail: 1200 x 1600 px : artwork with logo
    • Game Logo: 350 x 100 px (transparent background)
    • Mobile image: 1200 x1600 px: artwork without logo
  • Format: PNG, 1mb max
  • Copy (optional): 98 characters, including spaces
  • For a full list of resources regarding branding information for Epic Games Store, review the Brand Guidelines Overview.
Featured Carousel Specs: Option 2: Video Loop

  • Carousel video loop - 7.2 seconds

    Videos should NOT include ratings. Ratings will be added by the Epic Games Store media service overlay.

  • Videos should be 1920px width x 1080px height.

  • Videos should be uploaded as MP4 and not exceed 200MB. The MP4s will be converted via video profiles to support a broad array of devices: MP4, web (vp8), and HLS. Additional support is planned for future iterations.

  • For uploaded videos, please provide a video cover image that is also 1920px width x 1080px height and does not exceed 1MB. Cover images are important for videos viewed via mobile devices. If no specific cover image is provided, the product landscape and product horizontal image will be used as default.

  • Video duration should not exceed eight seconds.

  • Your video duration may be read differently depending on your operating system and video player. We have added a buffer of +1 seconds to account for this.

FAQ

Q: I filled the forms and provided the requested assets and copy - how can I make sure my content will be approved and will be online on time for my key beat?

A: The EGS Marketing team works to optimize all releases and commercial beats when submitted. Due to volume, we can't always offer placement and/or support on owned channels. Please remember submission to these forms do not guarantee support. If revisions are needed to copy or assets, we will reach out to the contact listed on the submission.

Q: What happens if I submit my request after the deadline?

A: In most cases, this will result in a delay of promotion. We will fast-track where possible but we may not be able to support your request in your desired timeframe.

Q: For Storefront Merchandising, which languages will my copy be localized to?

A: Unless otherwise specified, all above requests will be localized in Arabic, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Portuguese (Brazilian), Russian, Spanish (Spain), Spanish (Mexico), Thai, and Turkish. Regional restrictions may apply.
