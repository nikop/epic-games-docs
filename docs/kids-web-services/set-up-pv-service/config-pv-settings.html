Start your PV Service configuration

How to start setting up the PV service on your product.

Under 1 min to read

To select the PV service for your product:

  1. In the Dashboard, click on the required product, and then click the Services tab.

  2. In the Parent Verification panel, click View and edit:

    Click image for full size.

    The PV service settings are displayed. Now you can start configuring the service for the selected product.

The next step is to choose which verification methods will be available to parents (subject to region) in the verification webview.
