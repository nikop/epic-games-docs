What is the Email Subscriber List?

When Epic Games Store users purchase a product , they can opt in to email notifications about that product. At any time, you can request the list of email addresses of users who have opted in to receive notifications about your product.

Why Collect Email Subscribers?

When you gather the email addresses from users of your product, you gain a valuable opportunity to keep them informed about future product updates and discounts. When users actively provide their email addresses, they indicate their interest in your product and their desire to stay informed.

You can now directly reach these users through your existing newsletter or email campaigns.

In addition, it's an excellent opportunity to introduce this audience to other games in the same franchise, or similar products you offer that are likely to grab their interest.

Prerequisites

In order to receive your email marketing subscriber list:

  • Your organization must accept the Epic Games Store - Email Sharing Agreement. For more information, see Manage Agreements.
  • Your organization must provide a valid privacy policy to the Epic Games Store. If you do not have a privacy policy, the Epic Games Store does not collect email addresses.
    • If you did not provide a privacy policy when you first created your organization, you can add one in Dev Portal > Organization > Settings > General Settings > Edit general settings > Privacy Policy URL. If you need additional assistance, create a private discussion on our support website: eoshelp.epicgames.com.
  • Your organization must sign the most recent DPA (Data Protection Agreement). For more information, see Manage Agreements.
  • If you do not see some or any of these options, you might be using a different version of the Epic Games Store publishing tools. Reach out to your Release Management team for more information.

Request Process

To request your email subscriber marketing list:

  1. Go to our Developer Support website.
  2. Click CREATE PRIVATE DISCUSSION.
  3. Set the subject to Email Opt-In Delivery Request.

Note:

  • Requests typically take up to three days to fulfill.
  • We send the list to you in an encrypted email.

More Information

We send the full history of opt-ins for your product since collection began to you in an encrypted email.

For each opt-in, we log the email address, preferred language, and product name.

You must track opt-out requests independently from this list. We can only provide a history of Epic Games Store users who have ever opted in to receive updates about your product.

The opt-in is only available for products that are in a live state (in other words, they are available to purchase or play for free). It is not available to products that are in a "coming soon" state.
