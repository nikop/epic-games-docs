Get Started Overview

Overview of the Epic Games Store distribution process.

Epic Games Store Only
5 mins to read

Introduction

After you create your account on the Epic Games Developer Portal (Dev Portal), you are ready to get started!

An overview of the onboarding phase and the requirements for distribution are provided below, along with a recommended workflow for using the Epic Games Store publishing tools. As you begin, review this information (and the provided additional resources) to understand what you can expect throughout the distribution process.

Important Note: By default, new organizations can follow the standard onboarding process (outlined below) to access the Epic Games Store publishing tools. If you created your organization prior to March 9, 2023, review these next steps to learn more about gaining access to the publishing tools.

Onboarding Process

The onboarding phase is the first step towards distributing your product on the Epic Games Store.

During onboarding, you will set up your organization in the Dev Portal and complete a few required tasks (listed below). Once these tasks are complete, your organization receives access to the Epic Games Store publishing tools.

  • Epic Games Store Agreements: In order to use the publishing tools, a legal signatory for your organization must accept the Epic Games Store Distribution agreement. Depending on your organization's unique needs, additional agreements (such as the Email Sharing Rider) may be needed.
  • Submission Fee: A submission fee of $100 USD (or local currency equivalent), plus local taxes if applicable, is required for each product you intend to distribute. You are prompted to provide this fee during the onboarding process in the Dev Portal.
    • Note: While this fee is non-refundable, it is recoupable in the payment made after your product has earned at least $1000.00 in revenue from Epic Games Store purchases (and in-game purchases using the Epic Payment Platform). If you have any questions about the submission fee, create a private discussion on our Developer Support site for assistance.
  • Product Questionnaire: During the onboarding process in the Dev Portal, you are prompted to complete a preliminary questionnaire about your product. This questionnaire helps the Epic Games Store team identify how to best support your product during the distribution process. While completing the questionnaire is required, your responses are non-binding.

Learn more: Onboarding Phase

In the Dev Portal, you will also complete a brief tax interview form and set up your payout account. Prepare your identity, tax, and payout details as your organization's information will undergo verification. This process can take up to 2 weeks to complete and is required in order to submit your product for final review prior to launch.

Learn more: Manage Tax and Payout Details

Requirements & Guidelines

Before using the Epic Games Store publishing tools, we strongly recommend reviewing our requirements and guidelines for distributing products on the storefront. Reviewing these policies early in the process ensures you understand the expectations and criteria that must be met in order to launch your product.

Distribution Requirements

To promote a trustworthy and consistent experience for users, the Epic Games Store maintains a number of requirements for distribution. When your product is reviewed by the Epic Games Store team prior to launch, we will verify that your submission is complete and compliant with these policies.

Requirements for distribution include the following:

  • Products featuring multiplayer functionality must support crossplay across all PC storefronts where the product is distributed.
  • Products that offer achievements on other PC storefronts that sell 3rd-party products must have Epic Games Store achievements enabled.
  • Products with in-app purchases must disclose that information on their product page in the storefront.
  • Products leveraging blockchain technology (cryptocurrency, NFT, etc.) must follow specific guidelines to provide transparency to users.

Learn more: Epic Games Store Requirements

Ratings

The Epic Games Store does not require products to be rated. However, ratings are required in the following circumstances:

  • Some regions require ratings from specific authorities in order to distribute products in those areas. Such regions include South Korea, Australia, Brazil, and Russia.
  • Products that have received ratings from any official rating authority must display that information on their product page in the storefront.
  • Products leveraging blockchain or NFT technology in any capacity must be rated in order to be distributed on the Epic Games Store.

While you can distribute your product as an unrated title in regions without specific requirements, be aware that unrated products are treated as having the highest-level maturity rating for each region where they are distributed.

To best reach potential users on the storefront, obtaining ratings for your product is highly recommended. If you do not have ratings for your product, you can quickly obtain an IARC rating through the Epic Games Store publishing tools.

Learn more: Ratings on the Epic Games Store

Content Guidelines

The Epic Games Store maintains a set of content guidelines for products on the storefront. These guidelines cover a variety of topics, including:

  • Storefront content
  • Prohibited content
  • Linked-to and user-generated content
  • Localization

Using the Publishing Tools

The Epic Games Store publishing tools provide you with everything you need to distribute your product on the Epic Games Store. To ensure a successful launch of your product, our recommended workflow includes the following phases:

  • Onboarding: To get started, set up your organization, complete the required onboarding tasks, and provide your tax and payout information.
  • Publish Your Product Page (Early Awareness): If you have not yet released your product, we recommend publishing your product page at the same time as your product announcement to help spread early awareness. To complete this step, provide preliminary configurations for a variety of entities to create your store presence, such as product settings, ratings, and your base game offer.
  • Finalize and Test Configurations: Next, complete all the configurations needed to launch your product, such as uploading your build or creating additional offers. Then, conduct testing to ensure your product is ready for review by the Epic Games Store team.
  • Submit for Final Review: Prior to launch, submit your product for review. The Epic Games Store team will review your content and builds to ensure they meet our requirements and guidelines.
  • Launch Your Product: Once your product has been approved, you are ready to publish your product to the Epic Games Store!

Learn more: Publishing Tools Workflow

Next Steps

Now that you are more familiar with the distribution process, you are ready to get started with the Epic Games Store publishing tools!

From here, we recommend these next steps:

  • Review our Requirements & Guidelines to understand our policies and prepare for distribution.
  • Refer to the Publishing Tools Workflow for a detailed walkthrough of the distribution process.
  • Check out our Product Marketing Tips for general best practices around promoting your product on the storefront.
  • Gather the below information and complete the onboarding phase:
    • High-level details about your product or service, including its name and a short description
    • Payment information for the required submission fee
    • An individual who can complete the required agreements (in other words, the owner of your Dev Portal organization or a team member with Agreements Management permissions, previously called Legal Management permissions)
    • Individual or Company tax information, such as IDs
    • Banking information for where Epic Games Store payouts will be sent

If you have any questions or need assistance, reach out through the Epic Games Store Developer Community.
