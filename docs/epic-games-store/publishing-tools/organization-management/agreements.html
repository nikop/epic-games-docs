Manage Agreements

Review and accept Epic Games Store agreements

3 mins to read

Are you a Closed Beta partner? If you are not participating in the Closed Beta release, you can access documentation by using your Epic Games account to sign in to the Knowledge Base.

Overview

In order to distribute your product on the Epic Games Store, your organization owner must read and accept the appropriate agreements. You cannot use the Epic Games Store publishing tools until your organization owner completes this step.

  • You must be 18 or older to accept the required agreements and distribute your product on the Epic Games Store. If your organization owner is unable to accept agreements on behalf of your legal entity, contact support.
  • If Epic Games makes a major version update to the End User License agreement, your organization owner must accept the new agreement before you can continue using the publishing tools. You cannot use the publishing tools until the new agreement is complete.
  • When Epic Games updates a specific feature agreement that you have accepted, the Dev Portal notifies you upon your next attempt to access the publishing tools. Your organization owner must accept the updated agreement in order to maintain use of the associated feature.

Review and Accept Agreements

You can review the Epic Games Store agreements at any time through the Agreements section of the Dev Portal.

Note that this section contains a number of agreements related to both the Epic Games Store and other services offered by Epic Games. The following agreements are pertinent to the Epic Games Store publishing tools:

  • Epic Games Store Distribution agreement
    • Accepting this agreement is required in order to use the Epic Games Store publishing tools.
  • Epic Games Store Email Sharing agreement
    • Accepting this agreement is required in order to request and leverage the Email Marketing Subscriber List.

To review and accept an agreement, follow the steps below. This process can also be used to review or download agreements previously accepted by your organization.

  1. In the Dev Portal, navigate to Organization > Agreements.
  2. Locate the appropriate agreement and click Review.
  3. Review the terms of the agreement. You must view the entire agreement before it can be accepted.
  4. (Optional) To save an offline copy of the agreement, click Download Files on the right-side panel.
  5. To accept the terms after reviewing the agreement, click Accept.

Notes:

  • Agreements are only available only in English.
  • When you accept an agreement, your name and the timestamp for when you signed the agreement are recorded.
  • While any member of an organization can read an agreement, agreements can only be accepted by the organization owner. If your organization owner is unable to accept an agreement for any reason, contact support.

Review Your Organization’s Agreement History

The Agreements section of the Dev Portal also provides an overview of your organization’s historical interaction with each agreement. For each agreement, the following information is available:

  • The specific versions of the agreement that your organization has accepted.
  • The organization member who accepted each agreement version.
  • The date and time each agreement version was accepted.

To review a specific agreement's history, follow the steps below:

  1. Navigate to Organization > Agreements.
  2. Locate the appropriate agreement and click History.
  3. (Optional): To save a copy of a specific agreement version, click the Download icon within its row.

Note: To review the current terms of an agreement, follow the process for reviewing and accepting an agreement.
